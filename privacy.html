Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how KRVN Pipeline ("the Application") collects, uses, and protects information when you connect your TikTok account.

1. Information We Collect

When you authorize the Application via TikTok Login Kit, we may access:

  • Basic profile info: your TikTok display name and avatar (used only to confirm successful authentication)
  • Access tokens: OAuth tokens required to post content on your behalf

We do not collect passwords, payment information, or any other personal data.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account
  • Publish scheduled video content to your TikTok profile via the Content Posting API

We do not use your information for advertising, analytics, or any other purpose.

3. Data Storage

Access tokens are stored locally on the operator's device (never on cloud servers). No personal data is transmitted to or stored on third-party servers.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your information with any third parties. Data is only transmitted to TikTok's official API endpoints as required to publish content.

5. Data Retention

Access tokens are retained only as long as needed to operate the Application. You can revoke access at any time via TikTok account settings → Manage app permissions.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke the Application's access to your TikTok account at any time
  • Request deletion of any locally stored tokens by contacting us

7. TikTok's Privacy Policy

Content published through this Application is subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related questions: contact.big.appli@gmail.com
